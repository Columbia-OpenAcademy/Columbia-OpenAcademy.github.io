Open Academy at Columbia University

OpenAcademy is in its first year at Columbia University. OpenAcademy is a program that sets current college students up with mentors and projects in the open source community. Working mostly independently, but with teams around the world, Columbia students have been working all semester to bring fixes and features to real open source projects. Come by our presentation day to check them out!
